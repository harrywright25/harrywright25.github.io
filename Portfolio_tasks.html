Week 8

An argument made at the festival of economics was that Russian oil brings 5-7 times more revenue than gas. Below are three charts of oil and gas rents:

It can be seen that oil rents range between 2-3.5 times more than natural gas rents in Russia. Rents are estimated differences between the price and cost of the commodity. This implies that oil does not bring 5-7 times more revenue, unless the cost of producing gas is far higher than oil.
